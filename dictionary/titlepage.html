Tsugaru Dialect Learner's Dictionary



Mikoto Ono

Hirosaki University






The Tsugaru Dialect Learner's Dictionary is a dictionary for the Tusgaru dialect. Words are presented with romanization and Japanese script, together with:

  • word class information
  • sense definitions
  • pronunciation variations
  • synonyms
  • pictures
  • entity information for nouns
  • examples to demonstrate word use

The romanization is a revised version of Hepburn Romanization that aims to reflect the pronunciation of the Tsugaru dialect in a way that is easier for second language learners of Japanese to understand.

The sense definitions follow the COBUILD (learner dictionary) method of embedding headwords into English sentences that both define and illustrate word use.

The examples are drawn from a dialect corpus, The Matsunoki Treebank. This is a collection of transcriptions from recordings of monologue readings of Japanese folktales. The recordings are from performances by the “Wa no Mukashi Ko” group of storytellers who are active in Hirosaki City and surrounding areas.

The examples are presented with clickable links to the dialect corpus. In addition to reaching the full context for examples, following the corpus links offers the opportunity to search and explore parsed linguistic tree-based analysis. There are also English translations crafted to follow the linguistic analysis. These translations serve as an aid for language learners and as comments to assist understanding the linguistic analysis.
